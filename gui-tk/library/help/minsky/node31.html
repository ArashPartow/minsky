Entering accounts

Double click or right click on the Godley table block to bring up the Godley Table.

When a Godley Table is first loaded, it has room for one account to be defined. To create an additional accounts, click on the `+' button above the first account. One click then adds another column in which an additional account can be defined.

Flows / Stock Variables
  noAssetClass
Initial Conditions

A column can be deleted by clicking on the `-' button above the column.

To define bank accounts in the system you enter a name into the row labeled “Flows V / Stock Variables ->”.
