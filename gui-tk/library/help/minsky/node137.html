Trigonometry Functions

FUNCTION DEFINITION
acos Arc cosine of x expressed in radians. Interval (eg: acos(x))
acosh Inverse hyperbolic cosine of x expressed in radians. (eg: acosh(x))
asin Arc sine of x expressed in radians. Interval (eg: asin(x))
asinh Inverse hyperbolic sine of x expressed in radians. (eg: asinh(x))
atan Arc tangent of x expressed in radians. Interval (eg: atan(x))
atan2 Arc tangent of expressed in radians. (eg: atan2(x,y))
atanh Inverse hyperbolic tangent of expressed in radians. (eg: atanh(x))
cos Cosine of . (eg: cos(x))
cosh Hyperbolic cosine of . (eg: cosh(x))
cot Cotangent of . (eg: cot(x))
csc Cosecant of . (eg: csc(x))
sec Secant of . (eg: sec(x))
sin Sine of . (eg: sin(x))
sinc Sine cardinal of . (eg: sinc(x))
sinh Hyperbolic sine of . (eg: sinh(x))
tan Tangent of . (eg: tan(x))
tanh Hyperbolic tangent of . (eg: tanh(x))
deg2rad Convert from degrees to radians. (eg: deg2rad(x))
deg2grad Convert from degrees to gradians. (eg: deg2grad(x))
rad2deg Convert from radians to degrees. (eg: rad2deg(x))
grad2deg Convert from gradians to degrees. (eg: grad2deg(x))
