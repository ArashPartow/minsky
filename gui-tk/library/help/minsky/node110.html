Wires >>>>>>> Graeber

Wire represent the flow of values from one operation to the next. To add a wire to the canvas, click on the output port of an operation or variable (right hand side of the icon in its initial unrotated orientation), and then drag it towards an input port (on the left hand side of an unrotated icon). You can't connect an operator to itself (that would be a loop, which is not allowed, unless passing through an integral), nor can an input port have more than one wire attached, with the exception of and , where the multiple wires are summed or multiplied, respectively, and similarly max/min.
