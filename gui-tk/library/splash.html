Change to Minsky distribution

This is the very last version of Minsky for which Windows and MacOSX builds will be available for free on SourceForge and Github.

Future Windows and MacOSX builds will be available by signing up on Patreon for as little as US $1 per month. See what's changed since this release.

We remain committed to open source, and Minsky will always be open source. You are most welcome to download the source code and build it yourself. The binary packages are a convenience service for those who'd rather spend their time doing something else.
